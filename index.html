About

A Journey Shaped by Faith, Mission, and Scholarship. Rev. Dr. Vinod S. Sailus serves as Professor of Missiology at St. Peter’s Pontifical Institute, Bengaluru, integrating intercultural theology, mission praxis, and contextual Christian witness.

Timeline

1980 — Born in Kerala
2004–2008 — BD, Gurukul Lutheran Theological College
2009 — Ordained Presbyter, CSI
2013–2015 — M.Th Missiology, UTC
2016–2019 — D.Th Studies, UTC
2021–2025 — Associate Professor & HoD, Gurukul
2025–Present — Professor, St. Peter’s Pontifical Institute

Education
