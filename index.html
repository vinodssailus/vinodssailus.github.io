Rev. Dr. Vinod S. Sailus

Profile

Ordained Presbyter of CSI South Kerala Diocese and Professor of Missiology at St. Peter’s Pontifical Institute with over three decades of ministerial, academic, and mission experience. Scholar in intercultural theology, contextual mission, and interfaith engagement. Author and editor of multiple books and numerous academic publications with global theological participation.

Education & Formation

  • BA History – Christian College Kattakada
  • BD – Gurukul Lutheran Theological College (Academic Prize)
  • M.Th Missiology – United Theological College (Second Rank)
  • D.Th Missiology – United Theological College

Ministerial Journey

1995–2002 — Early Church Ministry & VBS
2003–2004 — Missionary, Eturnagaram
2008–2011 — Principal & Pastorate Leadership
2011–2013 — Established Mission Field, Bhadrachalam
2021–2025 — Associate Professor & HoD, Gurukul
2025–Present — Professor, St. Peter’s Pontifical Institute

Books & Publications

Author and editor of major works including Borderless God, Borderless Spirituality, The Development of Missiological Thinking and Praxis, The Dynamics of Mission in Transition, and Doing Mission with Muslims, alongside extensive journal and book chapter contributions.

Research & Academic Supervision

Supervision of D.Min., PhD, MTh, and BD scholars; research in intercultural theology, sacred spaces, mission praxis, and interfaith dialogue. Doctoral dissertation on Shared Sacred Places and Intercultural Mission.

International & National Conferences

Presented papers in multiple international forums including University of Oldenburg Winter School, University of Münster Summer School, Christian Conference of Asia Congress, and national missiological seminars across India.

Awards & Academic Prizes

  • College Special Prize for Missiology – Gurukul (BD)
  • Biakveli & Laldailova Pachuau Prize – UTC (MTh)
  • Ajith Sandeep Paul Memorial Prize – UTC (Second Rank MTh)

Family

Married to Vinitha V. R., with children Aleph V. Vinod, Ankith V. Vinod, and Aadhish V. Vinod. Family life forms a vital spiritual foundation within his vocation and ministry.
