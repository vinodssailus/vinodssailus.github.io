Profile Description

I am an ordained Presbyter of the CSI South Kerala Diocese and currently serve as Professor of Missiology at St. Peter’s Pontifical Seminary, Bangalore, with over thirty years of experience in ordained ministry, mission, and theological education. I served as Head of the Department of Missiology at Gurukul Lutheran Theological College for four years and received three academic special prizes in Missiology during my BD and MTh studies. I have published nine books (authored and edited) and thirty-two academic articles and reviews, with five forthcoming publications, and have presented papers at five international and twelve national conferences. I have four years of MTh teaching experience and have supervised MTh, BD, DMin, and PhD scholars under recognised institutions. My field experience includes seven years as a full-time missionary in Telangana, service as Principal of CSI Mission High Schools, work as Telangana State Mission Coordinator, and participation in establishing a new mission field in Bhadrachalam under the Dornakal Diocese. I have sixteen years of experience as an ordained Presbyter in the CSI and am proficient in English, Malayalam, Telugu, and Tamil, with professional training in Hebrew, Greek, German, and Telugu.
