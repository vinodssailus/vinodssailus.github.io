Profile Description

I am an ordained Presbyter of the CSI South Kerala Diocese and currently serve as Professor of Missiology at St. Peter’s Pontifical Seminary, Bangalore, with over thirty years of experience in ordained ministry, mission, and theological education. My ministry integrates academic leadership, intercultural theology, research, and pastoral engagement across India and international contexts.
