About

Rev. Dr. Vinod S. Sailus is an ordained Presbyter of the Church of South India and Professor of Missiology at St. Peter’s Pontifical Institute, Bengaluru. His ministry integrates pastoral leadership, intercultural theology, and scholarly engagement within the pluralistic realities of contemporary India and global Christianity.

Timeline

2009 — Ordained Presbyter
2013–2015 — M.Th. Missiology, UTC
